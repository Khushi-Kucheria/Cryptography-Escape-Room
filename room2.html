Vigenère Cipher is a polyalphabetic cipher. Each letter is shifted by the letter in the repeated keyword.
The keyword repeats to match the message length.
To decrypt, shift each letter of the ciphertext backwards by the alphabetical index of the corresponding keyword letter.
(A=0, B=1, ..., Z=25)
